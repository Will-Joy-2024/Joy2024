Lightning Thief

Book Review, Feb 29, 2024

By Rick Riordan

The Lightning Thief is a action packed fantasy book about Percy Jackson, who is 12-year-old boy and learns that his father is acuatlly Poseidon, the Greek god of water. He sets out to find his father and zeus's lightning bolt. During his journey he sets off with his friends that he made at camp, Grover and Anabeth.I think that they add a nice change to the story because he has to make the decison of saving his friends or his family.Riordan draws the reader in through the different plot twsits and action.
